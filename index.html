Who am I?

Hey there, I'm Ryan! a first year computer science student at National University Manila. My love for programming was first ignited when I was in senior high school. I remember back then I love reading manga a lot, thus finding a really nice platform with a nice ui/ux and reading experience was a must for me. It was then that I discovered this open source android app called Tachiyomi. It was still relatively new back then, or so I think, and every new features being released excited me and even joined their discord server to keep up with the update. Seeing them folks talk about programming fascinated me. I installed an android app that allowed me to customized the color of Tachiyomi a little bit. I changed the accent color into something I want and I felt really amazing like "Im a programmer now?".

That was the time that I pursued programming seriously and my journey with it has begun.
